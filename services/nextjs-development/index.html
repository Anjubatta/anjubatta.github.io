Frequently Asked Questions

What is SSR and SSG? SSR renders pages on each request (fresh content), SSG pre-renders pages at build time (faster). Both are SEO-friendly.

Is Next.js good for SEO? Yes, Next.js is built for SEO with metadata, structured data, sitemaps, and server-side rendering.

Can I build e-commerce with Next.js? Yes, with product pages, cart, checkout, and payment integration.
