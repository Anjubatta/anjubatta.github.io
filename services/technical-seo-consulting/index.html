Why Technical SEO Matters

Most Ranking Problems Are Technical, Not Content Problems

After analyzing 500+ websites, I can tell you with certainty: most ranking problems aren't due to bad content. They're due to technical infrastructure issues that prevent Google from properly crawling, understanding, and indexing your pages.

A perfectly written article that Google can't crawl will rank for zero keywords. A mediocre article that's properly optimized for crawl budget, Core Web Vitals, and schema will outrank competitors. Technical SEO is the foundation.

The reality: 70% of ranking improvements come from fixing technical SEO issues. Only 30% come from content creation. Yet most websites spend 100% of their effort on content and 0% on technical SEO.

Our Services

What Gets Fixed in a Technical SEO Audit

🕷️

Crawlability & Crawl Budget

Fix robots.txt blocking, redirect chains, server errors, and crawl budget wastage. Ensure Google can access and crawl all important pages efficiently.

📑

Indexation & Canonicalization

Remove duplicate content signals, fix canonical tags, eliminate accidental noindex tags, and ensure only your preferred URLs are indexed.

⚡

Core Web Vitals Optimization

Improve LCP (Largest Contentful Paint), CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift), and INP (Interaction to Next Paint) to meet Google's ranking requirements.

📊

Schema Markup & Structured Data

Implement proper JSON-LD schema, fix validation errors, and enable rich results for better search visibility and click-through rates.

🔗

JavaScript & Rendering Issues

Diagnose JavaScript rendering problems, ensure critical content is in the HTML source, and optimize for Google's JavaScript crawler.

📍

Site Structure & Internal Linking

Optimize your information architecture, improve crawl efficiency, and distribute page authority through strategic internal linking.
